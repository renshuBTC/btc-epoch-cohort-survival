Stratified Kaplan-Meier — Bitcoin new-buyer cohort survival by halving epoch

Five Kaplan-Meier survival curves, one per halving epoch. For each epoch, the cohort is the set of UTXOs born in the first W days post-halving and still un-spent at the close of that onboarding window. Each curve tracks the fraction of that cohort still un-spent through day (T − W) post-onboarding, where T = … days is how long Epoch 5 has been in progress. The chart extends by one day each calendar day — Epoch 5 always sits at the right edge, prior epochs are truncated to the same days-since-halving point for fair comparison.
